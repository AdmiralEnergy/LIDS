# PART 3: UNDERSTANDING YOUR NUMBERS

## 3.1 What Each Metric Tells You

### If Your Connect Rate is Low (<8%):
- **Possible Causes:**
  - Calling at wrong times
  - Phone number flagged as spam
  - Poor lead list quality
- **Solutions:**
  - Optimize call timing (Tues/Wed 10-11am best)
  - Rotate caller IDs
  - Audit lead source quality

### If Your Sub-30s Drop Rate is High (>70%):
- **Possible Causes:**
  - Opener sounds scripted/robotic
  - Not creating immediate relevance
  - Tone too enthusiastic or pushy
- **Solutions:**
  - Practice opener until natural
  - Lead with local relevance
  - Slow down, lower pitch

### If Your 2+ Minute Rate is Low (<15%):
- **Possible Causes:**
  - Not asking engaging questions
  - Talking too much, not listening
  - Missing curiosity hooks
- **Solutions:**
  - Ask more open-ended questions
  - Reduce talk ratio below 50%
  - Use curiosity-based discovery

### If Your Call-to-Appointment Rate is Low (<2%):
- **Possible Causes:**
  - Not clearly asking for the appointment
  - Poor qualification (wasting time on unqualified prospects)
  - Weak value proposition
- **Solutions:**
  - Always end with clear appointment ask
  - Qualify earlier in conversation
  - Strengthen pain/benefit articulation
