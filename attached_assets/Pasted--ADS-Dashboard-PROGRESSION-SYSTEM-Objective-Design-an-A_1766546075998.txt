# ADS Dashboard: PROGRESSION SYSTEM

**Objective:** Design an AAA video game-like progression system for Admiral Energy's sales reps that makes invisible growth visible, reduces 45-day dropout, and creates a meritocracy-based path from SDR to Sales Operative.

**Target Codebase:** `git@github.com:AdmiralEnergy/LIDS.git`

---

## Design Philosophy (Based on Analysis)

### From Octalysis Framework (Yu-kai Chou)
- **Current ADS Score:** 32/80 | **Target:** 58/80
- **Missing Core Drives:**
  - CD5: Social Influence (leaderboards, guild mechanics, mentorship)
  - CD3: Empowerment (creative problem-solving, branching paths)
  - CD7: Unpredictability (mystery boxes, variable rewards)
- **Balance:** White Hat (autonomy, meaning) > Black Hat (urgency, scarcity)
- **The Zynga Warning:** Avoid pure Black Hat - users leave with relief, not regret

### From David's Background (Navy, Power Home Remodeling)
- Military-style meritorious promotions (E-2 â†’ E-5)
- "Making invisible growth visible" - show reps their daily improvement
- 45-day dropout problem: reps quit before seeing results
- Servant leadership: make reps feel like they're winning

### Core Principle
> "A great game makes you feel skilled, not manipulated."
